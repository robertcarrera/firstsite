A Place Where Dogs Are Welcome

"Hi Dad! You have been neglecting me and cabin fever is setting in again. Do you remember what happened the last time you forgot about me? That's right. The bedroom door was left ajar and I went inside, hopped onto the bed, and discovered what fun it was to shred that down comforter into little pieces and scatter the feathers all over the room! I remember you were unimpressed, despite my best creative effort. Well, I was thinking, there is a solution to our problem. There's this beach my friends told me about where we could go, where I could wander about off leash! I could run and run, and get my pent up energy out. It sounds so wonderful. I can almost imagine it in my mind's eye. Can we go Dad? Please, oh please take me there. I promise I'll behave better when you leave me all alone at home."
